Once upon a time there was a beautiful lady. Her name was Margo. She had two sisters and they were all orphans. The youngest loved unicorns, especially ones that were big and fluffy. She always cried, "It's so big and fluffy I'm going to die!" Then Vector stole the Great Pyramid of Giza. The girls went to the theme park. They loved it, the girls got to play games and win unicorns. The end.
